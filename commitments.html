Our Pledge

More Than
Just Cards

Epic Decks is built around collecting, community and impact. Every month, a share of our sales goes to causes chosen by the community — with proof published here.

10%
Of Monthly Sales · Donated Every Month

Every month, 10% of our total sales is donated to a cause selected with help from the Epic Decks community. You buy from us — you help shape where the support goes.

🗳️

Community Votes

Our followers help decide which cause receives support. The goal is simple: make giving back part of the collecting experience.

We publish monthly donation records on this page so the community can see what was donated and where it went.
