5%
Of Monthly Stream Sales Donated

Every month, Epic Decks donates 5% of stream sales to organizations chosen with help from our community. On months with special occasions or special campaigns, we aim to increase this commitment to 10%.

🗳️

Vote During Streams

Join our commitment streams. During the first 30 minutes, a poll will be active so the community can choose from the organizations shortlisted that month.

🎥

Donations Made Live

At the end of each month, the donation is made during a live stream. Links to the relevant streams can be published here afterwards.

📊

Proof Published

Donation proof, totals, and supported organizations will be published on this website so our community can clearly track the impact.
