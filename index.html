Our Story

By a Fan,
For the Fans

Epic Decks was born from a simple obsession: finding the most remarkable cards in the Pokémon TCG universe and sharing them with a community that loves them just as much as we do.

We operate across the Netherlands, Belgium, Germany and the UK — sourcing from private and professional collectors. We aim to handled every collectable with collector-grade care.

Our collection comprises of slabs from the top grading companies ACE Grading, PSA and Beckett as well as unique graded cards from up coming grading companies across the globe. We don't just sell cards. We hunt them, grade them, and tell their story.

🔥 Hunt with Obsession

We source from across the globe and are always chasing the best cards that our customers love at the right price.

💎 Grade with Precision

ACE Grading for quality and speed. PSA just because. Beckett for precision collectors. We match the grader to the card, every time.

🌍 Sell with Integrity

Transparent pricing anchored to real market data. Offers accepted on all inventory. Community-first approach — your trust is worth more than any single sale.
